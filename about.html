About

What information can you find here?
  • Data: statistics on solar across Australia and how NSW compares to other states.
  • Installation Guides: step by step breakdown of how to get panels on your roof, mainly for Newcastle.
  • Technology Explained: Simple explanations of how solar panels and battery systems work to save you money.
  • Local Resources: Links to trusted providers and government programs available in the Hunter region with source information and references.
Solar for Newcastle

Newcastle has always been an energy city. We want to see our local community lead the way in the renewable energy revolution. By adopting solar, we aren't just saving money on bills we are protecting our beautiful coastline and ensuring a sustainable environment for the next generation and trying to stop coal.

Commitment to Quality

my website is committed to providing information that is accurate and easy to use and follow. All our data comes from reliable Australian energy sources and government website, and our guides are designed to be accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background.
