Solar Energy in Newcastle

Solar Energy in the Hunter Region

Newcastle's Renewable Transition

Newcastle has a long history with coal and energy production. Today, our city is at the forefront of the transition to renewable energy. With an average of over 200 sunny days a year, Newcastle is perfectly positioned to lead NSW in solar adoption, reducing our carbon footprint while supporting local green jobs.

Environmental Benefits for our Coast

Using solar power reduces the demand for fossil fuels, which helps protect our beautiful Newcastle beaches and the Hunter River from the long-term effects of climate change. Every kilowatt-hour of solar energy produced is a step toward preserving our local environment for future generations.

Economic Savings for Families

With electricity prices rising across New South Wales, solar energy offers a way for Newcastle families to take control of their bills. A typical residential solar system can pay for itself in 3 to 5 years, providing free electricity for decades and increasing the property value of your home.

Government Support & Rebates

The Australian Government provides significant incentives through Small-scale Technology Certificates (STCs). These function as an up-front discount on the cost of your solar system. Additionally, NSW residents may be eligible for specific state-based programs to make battery storage more affordable.
