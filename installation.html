A Simple Guide to Getting Solar

Follow these easy steps:

  • Look at your old bills: Check how much power your house uses. This tells you how many panels you might need.
  • Check your roof: Make sure your roof isn't covered in shade from big trees. In Newcastle, the best spot for panels is facing North because that's where the most sun is and if your are covered by trees they might refuse to install the solar pannels.
  • Find a good worker: Make sure the person putting the panels on your house has a special license. This means they are pros and knows what they are doing.
  • Ask for three prices: Don't just pick the first person you find. Ask different companies in Newcastle for a quote so you can see who has the best deals and supports you and you rhome the best.
  • One day of work: It usually only takes one day to put everything on your roof. You don't even need to leave your house while they do it.
  • Start saving: Once it's all plugged in, your house will start using the sun's power it is beneficial to buy it from the grid becasue it saves money
